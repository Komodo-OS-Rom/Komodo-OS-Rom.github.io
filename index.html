Frequently Asked Questions

Pertanyaan yang sering di ulang-ulang

  • What is being wiped during a ROM update?

    No need to wipe, this means you can do dirty flash.

  • Does this rom already include gapps?

    Komodo does not include gapps, because we provide the best options for microg users and gapps users can choose the preferred variant.

  • How to install GCam?

    Cam2api is active, please install GCam according to your device.

  • How to install the Komodo os?

    Clean flash recommended.

  • What is the difference between clean flash and dirty flash?

    Clean flash is recommended to do a wipe (system, data, cache, dalvink, vendor). While dirty flash is that you don't need to wipe anything but you are allowed to wipe cache and dalvink.

    You can make donations through PayPal, OVO, Dana.
